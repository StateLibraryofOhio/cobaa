About COBAA

The Celebrating Ohio Book Awards & Authors (COBAA) grant is a federally funded initiative administered by the State Library of Ohio. It supports Ohio libraries in developing their collections by providing up to $2,000 to purchase books by Ohio authors and titles recognized by Ohio-based book award programs. Since 2016, COBAA has helped connect readers with local voices and award-winning literature, enriching communities and promoting literacy across the state.

To learn more about the grant, eligibility, and how to apply, visit the State Library of Ohio COBAA page.
